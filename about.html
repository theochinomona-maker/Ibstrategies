Who We Are

Our Story

Integrated Business Strategies (IBS) is a South African consulting firm dedicated to partnering with organizations to drive operational excellence, compliance, and sustainable growth. Located in Midrand, IBS specializes in tailor-made process improvement solutions across a range of industries, from engineering to risk management and corporate training.

Our seasoned team brings expertise in SHREQ (Safety, Health, Environmental, Risk, and Quality) management, technical and civil engineering consulting, as well as continual professional development through comprehensive training programs. We pride ourselves on delivering hands-on, integrated solutions that empower businesses to meet regulatory requirements, enhance efficiency, and build robust infrastructures for the future.

At IBS, our mission is to help clients unlock their full potential by leveraging proven methodologies, local knowledge, and a commitment to continual improvement. Whether you are seeking expert guidance for your next major project, process optimization, or skills development, we are ready to deliver results that matter.
